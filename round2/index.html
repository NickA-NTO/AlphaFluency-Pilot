AlphaFluency Pilot Report

Round 2  ·  40 Students  ·  5 Campuses  ·  Grades K–2  ·  Alpha Schools  ·  March 2026
● Executive Summary

Across 40 students and 5 campuses, AlphaFluency showed a strong internal growth signal.

•  89% of students with 20+ minutes of use (25 of 28) showed measurable fluency gains.

•  Students are reading harder text faster than when they started. Among those with 2+ weeks and 30+ minutes on the programme, average reading difficulty increased by 1.5 ATOS levels while raw WCPM rose by +13 WCPM.

•  Post-pilot cold-read re-tests project approximately +3 WCPM per week in verified reading fluency.
