AlphaFluency Pilot Report

Round 2  ·  40 Students  ·  5 Campuses  ·  Grades K–2  ·  Alpha Schools  ·  March 2026
● Executive Summary
Across 40 students at 5 campuses, AlphaFluency showed a strong internal growth signal — 25 of 32 students with sufficient session time recorded positive normalised WCPM gains, averaging +54 adjusted WCPM. With most students fewer than two weeks into the programme, 13 of 30 assessable students have reached near-target reading difficulty, with the remainder still in active progression. A post-pilot Literably cold-read assessment is required to confirm these gains transfer to independent reading.
